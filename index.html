NeuroQuiet Ingredients

Below we have discussed the core ingredients present in the supplement in detail:

  1. Alpha-GPC:  Known for its cognitive benefits, Alpha-GPC has been shown in studies to improve brain function, memory, and learning capacity, making it a popular choice for cognitive support.
  2. Moomiyo: Moomiyo, often referred to as “Shilajit,” is a natural substance found in the Himalayas and other mountainous regions. It is celebrated for its adaptogenic properties, helping the body adapt to stress while enhancing vitality. Moomiyo is rich in minerals and antioxidants, contributing to both brain and body wellness.
  3. L-Arginine: L-Arginine significantly promotes healthy blood circulation, which is essential for optimal ear health and brain function. As an amino acid, it is a precursor to nitric oxide, a compound that helps relax blood vessels and improve circulation.
  4. L-Dopa Bean: L-Dopa Bean, derived from the Mucuna pruriens plant, is renowned for its ability to support dopamine production. Dopamine is a crucial neurotransmitter involved in mood regulation and cognitive functions. By enhancing dopamine levels, L-Dopa Bean helps reduce ear ringing associated with tinnitus, offering relief to those affected by this condition.
  5. GABA: GABA (Gamma-Aminobutyric Acid) is a naturally occurring neurotransmitter that plays a pivotal role in promoting relaxation and reducing stress levels. By inhibiting excessive neuronal firing, GABA helps to calm the mind and body, making it easier to manage anxiety and stress. This calming effect can lead to improved sleep quality, which is essential for overall health and cognitive function.
  6. L-Tyrosine: L-Tyrosine is another amino acid that supports stress resilience and neurotransmitter balance. Under stress, the body uses more neurotransmitters, which can lead to cognitive fatigue and mood swings. By supplementing with L-Tyrosine, users can enhance their ability to cope with stress and maintain cognitive performance.
